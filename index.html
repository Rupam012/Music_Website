As of my last update in September 2021, the Indian music industry is a vibrant and diverse landscape that encompasses various genres and styles. Bollywood music refers to the music used in Indian films and is a fusion of traditional and contemporary elements. Apart from Bollywood, there are regional music industries that produce music in languages such as Tamil, Telugu, Punjabi, Bengali, and more.
